Classes

1-2-1 or Group Mat Pilates

Mat Pilates classes are a floor-based workout that focuses on core strength, flexibility, posture, and overall body control. Using only a mat and sometimes small props (such as resistance bands, balls, or rings), you will perform a series of controlled movements that enhance stability, balance, and muscular endurance.

About

Meet Natasha Hastings

I have recently transitioned from a successful career in IT sales to pursue my passion for Pilates and fitness. I took up Pilates to keep physically and mentally healthy during lockdown, and immediately fell in love with home workouts. I quickly noticed the benefits Pilates workouts had in my running form, flexibilty, strength and recovery and soon discovered I wanted to help others by showing them the benefits of this method of exercise.

I moved from Buckinghamshire to Camberley, Surrey with my partner over 3 years ago. We have a beautiful rescue dog from Turkey named Benjy who keeps us busy when we're not working - he can’t wait to meet you!

I am working on expanding my expertise in pre- and post-natal care, corrective exercise, and full-equipment Pilates - keep an eye out for when this will be available at my home studio!
