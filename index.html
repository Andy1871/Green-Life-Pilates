About

Meet Natasha Hastinks

I have recently transitioned from a successful career in IT sales to pursue my passion for Pilates and fitness. I have been doing Pilates since lockdown and fell in love with at home work outs, taking up running shortly after I immediately noticed the difference and benefits in my form, flexibilty strength and balance seeing me through marathon training.

I am working on expanding my expertise in pre- and post-natal care, corrective exercise, and full-equipment Pilates, soon to all be offered at my home studio.

I moved from Buckinghamshire to Camberley with my partner over 3 years ago and we have a beautiful rescue dog from Turkey named Benjy who keeps us busy when not working, he can’t wait to meet you!
