Classes

1-2-1 Reformer Pilates

Private Reformer Pilates sessions in a fully-equipped private home studio located in Camberley, Surrey. Reformer pilates incorporates resistance to traditional pilates moves, providing a much deeper, full body workout. Sessions are welcome to all abilities, and will be personalised to each individual to provide the guidance and support neccessary for your fitness level, goals, and any specific needs or injuries.

Please use the button below to email me to book your reformer sessions. Block booking sessions will see a discount applied. All participants will be required to complete a short registration form prior to their first session.

Classes

1-2-1 Mat Pilates

Mat classes are the perfect introduction to the controlled movement of pilates. Using only a mat, and sometimes small props (such as resistance bands, balls, or rings), we focus on improving core strength, flexibility, posture, and overall body control.

1-2-1 mat sessions are available at a fully-equipped private studio and will be designed to support your individual needs. Use the button below to email me to book in your sessions. All participants will be required to complete a short registration form prior to their first session.

Group Mat Pilates

Larger group sessions are available at a community venue in Camberley, where we create a friendly, welcoming atmosphere. Group lessons are mixed abilities, catering for all ages and experience. You will be pushed, but also encouraged to work at a pace that suits you.

About

Meet Natasha Hastings

I have recently transitioned from a successful career in IT sales to pursue my passion for Pilates and fitness. I took up Pilates to keep physically and mentally healthy during lockdown, and immediately fell in love with home workouts. As a keen runner, I also soon noticed the benefits Pilates workouts had in my running form, flexibilty, strength and recovery.

I moved from Buckinghamshire to Camberley, Surrey with my partner over 3 years ago. We have a beautiful rescue dog from Turkey named Benjy who keeps us busy when we're not working - he can’t wait to meet you!

I am working on expanding my expertise in pre- and post-natal care, corrective exercise, and full-equipment Pilates - keep an eye out for when this will be available at my home studio!
