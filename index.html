Classes

1-2-1 Reformer Pilates

Private Reformer Pilates sessions in a fully-equipped home studio located in Camberley, Surrey. Reformer Pilates incorporates resistance to traditional Pilates moves, providing a much deeper, full body workout.

Sessions are welcome to all abilities, and will be personalised to each individual to provide the guidance and support necessary for your fitness level and goals.

Please use the button below to contact me to book your reformer sessions. Block booking sessions will see a discount applied. All participants will be required to complete a short registration form prior to their first session.

1-2-1 Mat Pilates

Mat classes are the perfect introduction to the controlled movement of Pilates. Using only a mat, and the occasional small prop (such as resistance bands, balls, or rings), you will focus on improving core strength, flexibility, posture, and overall body control.

1-2-1 mat sessions are available at a fully-equipped home studio and will be designed to support your individual needs. Use the button below to contact me to book in your sessions. All participants will be required to complete a short registration form prior to their first session.

Duo Pilates

Fancy a private Pilates session with a friend, family member or partner?

Duo Pilates gives you all the benefits of private Reformer and Mat Pilates, plus:

  • Increased motivation and support - I will push you to your limits every session. But you and your friend will motivate and drive each other even harder towards your goals.
  • Lower per-person cost - You will share the session and share the cost, but with the same focus on your Pilates as a 1-2-1 session. Your time will be split between the Mat and Reformer.

About

Meet Tasha

I have recently transitioned from a successful career in IT sales to pursue my passion for Pilates and fitness. I took up Pilates to keep physically and mentally healthy during lockdown, and immediately fell in love with home workouts. As a keen runner, I loved the benefits Pilates workouts had on my running form, flexibilty, strength and recovery.

I moved from Buckinghamshire to Camberley, Surrey with my partner over 3 years ago. We have a beautiful rescue dog from Turkey named Benjy who keeps us busy when we're not working - he can’t wait to meet you!
